About me

I am a Senior Associate Android Engineer at JPMorgan Chase, working on business banking features within the Chase Mobile App, including products such as QuickAccept and Invoicing that support financial workflows for millions of users.

I specialize in building modern Android applications using Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and architecture patterns such as MVVM/MVI, Clean Architecture, and modular design. My focus is on delivering high-performance, secure, and accessible mobile experiences in a production fintech environment.

Outside of work, I build personal projects to deepen my understanding of Android architecture and system design, including a habit tracking application with a custom backend built using a modern Android stack.
